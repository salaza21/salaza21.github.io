Site Description

This site will serve as the center hub for the description and operation of the weather assignment. It will provide descriptiion and purpose of the site.

Purpose Statement

The purpose of this webpage is to provide people from cities of northeastern Idaho the ability to prepare and plan their week without having to worry about the many weather changes that can happen in such a varied enviroment such as Idaho.
