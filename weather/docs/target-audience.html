Target Audience

The target audience is described here:

  • Who: People who live in the area and want to be informed of the weather events that may happen.
  • Age: 14 years and up.
  • Technology:
  • Income: All types of incomes, since weather does not discriminate.
  • Motivation: Anybody who would like to be prepared for the incoming day and the problems that the weather may bring.
