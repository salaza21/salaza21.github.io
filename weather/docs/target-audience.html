Target Audience

The target audience is described here:

  • Who: People who live in the area and want to be informed of the weather events that may happen.
  • Age: 14 years and up.
  • Technology:
  • Income: All types of incomes, since weather does not discriminate.
  • Motivation: Anybody who would like to be prepared for the incoming day and the problems that the weather may bring.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Why should I use this website?

  • Because you will find a an easy interface that will lead you directly to the best weather information for your city. Time matters and relying on the decisions for the coming day matter to you and to us.

Scenario 2: How can this website benefit me?

  • By giving you precise information, Weatheria will help your daily planning process more streamline and faster.

Scenario 3: What is the future of Wetheria?

  • In Weatheria we are not only just proud of providing information, but we are constantly looking for new ways to provide you with information in a more interactive manner. Enhances to menus, as well as more cities is something that we will always be looking for.

Scenario 4: How do I navigate this website?

  • Navigating this website is easy and intuitive. Just look for the city that you want to know the weather of in the top task bar and click it. You will recieve all the information that you need.
